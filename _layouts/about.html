ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Rachel Heo, a freelance UI & UX designer who is based in Vancouver and Seoul.

I have a passion for creating beautiful, intuitive, and highly crafted designs combining a considered approach with emerging technologies. I have wide ranging skills in designing from web and mobile interfaces to prints and logos.

My strengths lie in my attention to detail, the ability to refine complex requirements and information into simple and elegant solutions. I am not interested in simply “ticking the boxes” and churning out average designs. I want to make sure that everything I produce is something that I would be excited about if I were the client. I love what I do and I do what I love, and it makes me want to always outdo myself and seek out new challenges.
